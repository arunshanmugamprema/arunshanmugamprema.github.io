Featured Case Study

Redesigning Growth at Scale

FMCG · Sales Operations · Analytics · Supply Chain

The Problem

This is where things started breaking.

Broken incentives. No visibility. Decisions made on gut feel.

Retailer activation was stuck at 15%. Sales reps chased volume, not margin. Supply chain had no data — stock-outs were frequent, with no way to predict or prevent them. And buried in the billing system: over $2M in undetected errors that nobody had been looking for.

My Approach

So I redesigned the system from scratch.

Diagnose → Analyze → Redesign → Execute

→ Rebuilt incentive structure from volume-based to margin-based tiers — aligning what reps were rewarded for with what the business actually needed.

→ Built custom SQL analytics frameworks from scratch — automated reporting that used to take weeks now ran in hours.

→ Used holdout groups to measure true incremental lift and prove the system worked — not just felt better.

→ Coordinated rollout across Finance, Sales, Supply Chain, and IT simultaneously.

Then we shipped it — across 95 territories simultaneously.
