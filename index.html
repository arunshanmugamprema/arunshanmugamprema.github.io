Case Study · COVID Pivot · Bajaj Electricals

Zero-Budget Revenue Generation During Market Shutdown

Problem

Complete market shutdown in April 2020. Zero marketing budget. Traditional field sales and distribution channels unavailable. Regional peers posting negative growth.

Approach

Analyzed transaction data to identify 3 high-velocity micro-markets still showing demand signals. Reallocated field capacity entirely to those pockets. Activated digital outreach via WhatsApp with existing retailer relationships — no new spend.

Impact

₹16 Cr (~$1.9M) in revenue closed in a 90-day window with zero marketing budget — while regional peers posted negative growth. Pure data-driven resource reallocation.
